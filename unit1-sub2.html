Unit 1 - Reflection

1. What realizations did you gain regarding the importance of curriculum in education?

One realization I gained regarding the importance of curriculum in education is that it serves as the foundation of every learner’s growth and development. Curriculum is important because it gives direction to both teachers and students about what should be learned and achieved. As a student of Iloilo State University of Fisheries Science and Technology taking up Bachelor of Secondary Education major in English, I realized that curriculum does not only focus on academic knowledge but also helps students develop values, discipline, communication skills, and critical thinking. It shapes the learning experiences of students inside and outside the classroom. I also realized that curriculum helps prepare students for their future professions and responsibilities in society. Even though learning can sometimes be difficult and challenging, the curriculum guides us step by step toward becoming competent and responsible individuals. Through curriculum, education becomes organized, meaningful, and purposeful for every learner.

2. How does the curriculum influence your experiences as a learner?

The curriculum greatly influences my experiences as a learner because it determines the subjects, activities, teaching methods, and learning outcomes that I encounter in school. The curriculum helps me improve my communication skills, understanding of literature, and knowledge in teaching strategies that I can use in the future as an educator. However, I also experience challenges in learning, especially because I have a short attention span and sometimes find it hard to focus during discussions. Despite this difficulty, I continue trying my best to listen, participate, and understand the lessons because I know that these experiences are part of my growth as a future teacher. The curriculum encourages me to become more patient, determined, and responsible in my studies. It also teaches me the importance of perseverance and self-discipline in achieving my goals. Through these experiences, I realize that learning is not always easy, but it helps shape me into a better learner and future educator.
