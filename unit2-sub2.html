Unit 2 - Reflection

1. Which educational philosophy aligns most with your beliefs as a future teacher? Why?

The educational philosophy that aligns most with my beliefs as a future teacher is Progressivism. I believe that students learn better when they actively participate in discussions, activities, and real-life experiences instead of only listening to lectures. As a future teacher, I want to create a classroom where students feel encouraged to express their ideas, think critically, and learn through collaboration. I also believe that education should help students develop practical skills and values that they can use in everyday life.

2. Why should teachers understand learners' psychological and social needs?

Teachers should understand learners’ psychological and social needs because every student learns differently and faces different challenges. Some students may struggle with focus, confidence, family problems, or social interactions, which can affect their performance in school. When teachers understand these needs, they can adjust their teaching strategies and create a supportive learning environment for students. By showing patience, understanding, and encouragement, teachers can help learners feel motivated, valued, and more confident in their studies.
