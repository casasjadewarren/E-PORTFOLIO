LESSON PLAN

A.TOPIC:

Informational text in range of media (Documentary and video blog)

LEVEL:

GRADE 10 - QURTER 2

B.STANDARDS

After completing the documentary or video blog, students should be able to develop their communication and storytelling skills by presenting information in a clear, engaging, and organized manner. They will practice research and critical thinking by gathering reliable information, analyzing real-life issues, and drawing meaningful conclusions. The project also allows students to express their thoughts creatively and responsibly while exploring topics that highlight real-life experiences and important social concerns. In addition, students will learn to use media platforms ethically and purposefully to inform and inspire others. Through the planning, production, and presentation process, they will strengthen their collaboration and leadership skills by working effectively with others and taking active roles in completing the project successfully.

C.GOAL

The goal is to create a meaningful and informative documentary or video blog that explores a real-life issue, experience, culture, event, or social concern. The documentary or video blog should not only entertain viewers but also educate, inspire, and encourage reflection or awareness. Students are expected to research and develop a deep understanding of their chosen topic in order to present factual, accurate, and authentic information. They should go beyond simply reporting facts by sharing their own insights, observations, and relevant experiences that add meaning and perspective to the subject. Through the use of creative storytelling techniques, students should aim to engage viewers both emotionally and intellectually, making the presentation more compelling and memorable. Ultimately, their work should inspire viewers to think critically about the issue being discussed and, when appropriate, encourage them to take meaningful action.

D.ROLE

As a journalists, your goal is to investigate your chosen topic thoroughly and present factual, accurate, and authentic information through a documentary video blog. You are expected to use credible sources, thoughtful analysis, and creative storytelling techniques to inform and engage viewers both emotionally and intellectually. By sharing meaningful insights and real-life perspectives, your documentary should encourage the audience to think critically about the issue and inspire them to take informed and responsible action.

E.AUDIENCE

The target audience for the documentary or video blog includes classmates and teachers, who will assess the content and learn from the information presented. It may also reach parents, community members, and youth audiences who can better understand the significance of the topic and its impact on society. When shared on Facebook, the documentary has the potential to inform a wider audience, encourage meaningful discussions, and inspire viewers to reflect on the issue and take positive action.

F.SITUATION

In today’s generation, many people consume videos daily through social media platforms. However, not all content promotes awareness, truth, or meaningful reflection. Many important social, cultural, or community issues remain ignored or misunderstood. Your task is to create a documentary or video blog that highlights real-life stories, experiences, or concerns that deserve attention. It should be posted on a social media platform, Facebook. Through this project, you will use media and storytelling as tools to educate, influence, and connect with people. Possible topics for the documentary or video blog may include a wide range of real-life issues and meaningful stories that are relevant to students and society. These may focus on environmental problems, internet addiction and the influence of social media, mental health awareness, poverty and community struggles, and the pressures associated with student life and academics. Students may also explore local culture and traditions, acts of kindness and inspiring stories, as well as the challenges faced by workers and ordinary citizens. Each topic should provide an opportunity to present factual information, highlight authentic experiences, and encourage viewers to better understand important social issues and human experiences.

G.PRODUCT/PERFORMANCE

Students will produce a 5–10 minute documentary or video blog presented in video format that explores a real-life issue, experience, or story in an informative and engaging manner to be posted on Facebook. The documentary video blog may include an introduction to the topic, relevant background information, narration or voice-over, interviews, surveys, testimonies, actual footage or observations, as well as photos, video clips, music, and transitions to enhance the presentation. Students are also encouraged to include personal reflections or realizations and conclude their documentary with a meaningful summary or call to action. The documentary or video blog should follow a clear and organized structure. It begins with an introduction that creatively presents the topic, explains the purpose of the documentary, and captures the viewers’ attention through compelling visuals, questions, or statements. This is followed by the presentation of information, where students discuss the issue clearly and support their discussion with facts, examples, research findings, and real-life evidence. Interviews, surveys, or testimonies from individuals related to the topic should be included to provide authentic perspectives and enrich the content.

H.STANDARDS

The students will be grades by the following standard: Content and accuracy Organization and flow Creativity and originality Technical quality (audio, video editing, etc) Delivery and engagement
